Accommodation

The fee for the Accommodation includes a six-day, single-person-occupancy accommodation hosted by one of the Unimelb's College. Details are:

  • Host: Frappe Fort, University College, The University of Melbourne
  • Room Type: New College Ensuite Room (room details link)
  • Occupation: Single-person occupation only
  • Meals: Hot and continental breakfast included
  • Linen: Supplied by UC
  • Dates: Saturday 6th December - Friday 12th December 2025

The accommodation provide Additional catering (served in Dining Hall) but is charged by:

  • Lunch: AUD 21.50pp
  • Dinner:AUD 22.50pp
